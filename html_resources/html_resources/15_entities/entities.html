HTML5 Entities Example

Common HTML Entities

Here are some common HTML entities and their corresponding characters:

  • < represents <
  • > represents >
  • & represents &
  • “ represents “ (left double quotation mark)
  • ” represents ” (right double quotation mark)
  • © represents © (copyright symbol)
  • ™ represents ™ (trademark symbol)
  • € represents € (Euro symbol)

Mathematical and Special Characters

Entities can also represent mathematical and special characters:

α + β = γ

∞ means infinity.
