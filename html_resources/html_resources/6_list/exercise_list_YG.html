Rice and Chicken Recipe

This is the dish that has rice with chicken in it, the dish is one of kind and cannot be beat!

    Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Garlic
    Steps
  1. Heat Pan
  2. Season Chicken
  3. Cook Chicken
  4. Cook Rice
  5. Combine Rice and Chicken
  6. Enjoy!
